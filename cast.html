Cast and Characters

Henry Cavill plays the main character in the Witcher. He is Geralt of Rivia, a lonely witcher looking for his coin.

Freya Allan plays as Ciri in the Witcher. Ciri is a girl that was claimed by the Witcher with the law of surprise.

Anya Chalotra is playing as Yennefer, a mage that realizes she has a lot more power than she first thought.

Joey Batey is playing Jaskier. Jaskier is a humble barb that loves to play music at Geralt's side. He's good at making trouble
